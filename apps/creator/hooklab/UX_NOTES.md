# HookLab UX Notes

## Flow
1. **Hero + form:** Eyebrow `AI hook lab`, title `HookLab`, tagline about A/B testing. Form: idea textarea, platform select (YouTube/TikTok/Shorts), vibe slider or tags (Bold/Insightful/Fun), thumbnail keyword input.
2. **CTA:** `Generate Hook Set` validates idea, shows loader with color pulses.
3. **Results:** Highlight card recommending Hook A or B, variant cards (hook, metadata, copy buttons), thumbnail text card with char count, share row, upsell banner.
4. **Regenerate:** Button reuses prior inputs with new vibe.
5. **Error states:** Missing idea shows helper `Describe your video idea to generate hooks.`

## Wireframe
```
HookLab ⚡️
AI hook lab
[Idea textarea..........]

```

## Copy
- CTA: `Generate Hook Set`
- Loader: `Cooking your A/B hooks...`
- Highlight eyebrow: `Recommended variant`
- Variant labels: `Hook A`, `Hook B`
- Thumbnail header: `Thumbnail text`
- Share label: `Share with your editor`
- Upsell: `Unlock unlimited variants & saved templates`
